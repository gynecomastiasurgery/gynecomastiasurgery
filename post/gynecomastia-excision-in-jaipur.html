FAQs About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is not typically painful during the procedure itself, as patients are placed under anesthesia. However, it’s normal to experience some mild discomfort in the days following the surgery. This discomfort can be managed effectively with prescribed pain medications. Dr. Purohit’s team ensures that patients receive appropriate guidance on pain management strategies to minimize any discomfort during the recovery period.

Will there be visible scars after the procedure?

Dr. Purohit’s surgical expertise focuses on minimizing scarring and ensuring discreet results. While some scarring is inevitable, the placement of incisions is carefully planned to be as inconspicuous as possible. Over time, these scars usually fade and become less noticeable. Dr. Purohit also provides guidance on scar care and management to help patients achieve the best possible outcome in terms of scarring.

What is the typical recovery period?

The recovery period following gynecomastia surgery varies from person to person. Most patients can expect to resume light activities within a week after the surgery. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for a few weeks to ensure proper healing. Dr. Purohit’s team provides specific post-operative care instructions to support patients through their recovery journey.

Are the results permanent?

Gynecomastia surgery provides long-lasting results for most patients. The excess glandular tissue and fat that are removed during the surgery are typically gone permanently. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the longevity of the results. Weight fluctuations and hormonal changes can influence the chest’s appearance, so maintaining a stable weight and overall health is recommended.

Is gynecomastia surgery suitable for all body types?

Dr. Purohit’s approach to gynecomastia surgery is personalized and tailored to each patient’s unique needs and body type. Whether an individual has a lean build or a more robust physique, Dr. Purohit’s expertise allows him to adapt the surgical technique to achieve the best possible outcome for each patient. During the consultation, he evaluates factors such as tissue distribution and overall proportions to determine the most suitable treatment plan.
