FAQs: Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you embark on your journey towards gynecomastia surgery, it’s natural to have questions. Here are answers to some commonly asked questions to help you feel more informed and confident about your decision.

Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery discomfort is manageable and diminishes as you heal. Pain medication and post-operative instructions will be provided to ensure a smooth and comfortable recovery. While some level of discomfort is to be expected, our team is committed to minimizing any discomfort you might experience.

How soon can I resume regular activities after surgery?

The timeline for resuming regular activities varies depending on the extent of your surgery and your body’s healing process. Light activities can usually be resumed within a week, but strenuous exercises and heavy lifting should be avoided for a few weeks to ensure proper healing and optimal results.

Will there be visible scars after surgery?

Our emphasis on minimally invasive techniques helps minimize scarring. While some scarring is inevitable, the incisions are strategically placed to be as discreet as possible. Scars typically fade over time and can often be easily concealed, allowing you to confidently showcase your enhanced chest.

Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent, provided you maintain a healthy lifestyle. While the surgery removes excess breast tissue and fat, fluctuations in weight and hormonal changes can impact the results over time. However, a stable weight and healthy habits can help ensure the longevity of your new chest contour.

Is gynecomastia surgery covered by insurance?

In most cases, gynecomastia surgery is considered cosmetic and is not covered by insurance. It’s important to check with your insurance provider to understand their policies.
