FAQs: Common Questions About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

If you’re considering gynecomastia surgery, it’s natural to have questions. Below, I’ve addressed some of the most commonly asked questions to provide you with clear and accurate information about the procedure and its benefits.

1. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you won’t experience pain during the procedure. Post-surgery discomfort is manageable and typically subsides within a few days. Pain medications and post-operative care instructions will be provided to ensure a comfortable recovery.

2. How long is the recovery period after surgery?

Recovery time varies depending on individual factors, but generally ranges from one to two weeks. During this period, you may experience swelling and bruising, which are normal parts of the healing process. Supportive garments will aid in reducing swelling, and I will provide you with detailed guidelines to promote a smooth recovery.

3. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

I employ advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring and ensure discreet incisions. While some scarring may be inevitable, it tends to fade significantly over time. I strategically place incisions to achieve the best aesthetic outcome possible.

4. When can I resume physical activities after surgery?

Light activities can usually be resumed within about two weeks after surgery. However, strenuous exercises and activities that put pressure on the chest area should be avoided for about four to six weeks to allow for proper healing and optimal results.

5. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and weight is important to sustain your results over the long term. I will provide guidance on how to preserve your new chest contour for years to come.
