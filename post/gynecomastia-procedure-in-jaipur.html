Understanding True Gynecomastia

True gynecomastia refers to the enlargement of glandular breast tissue in males. It is often caused by hormonal imbalances, particularly an increase in estrogen levels relative to testosterone. This condition can be distressing, affecting self-esteem and causing discomfort. Dr. Purohit’s clinic provides a thorough evaluation to accurately diagnose true gynecomastia and determine the most appropriate treatment path.

1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

Recovery times can vary based on individual factors and the extent of the procedure. Generally, patients can anticipate returning to light activities within about a week. However, it’s advisable to avoid strenuous exercises and activities for a few weeks to ensure proper healing and optimal results.

2. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery is considered safe when performed by a skilled and experienced surgeon. Dr. Vishal Purohit’s expertise ensures that the procedure is conducted with precision and care, minimizing potential risks. Prior to the surgery, a comprehensive assessment is carried out to determine your eligibility and ensure that any underlying health concerns are addressed.

3. Will there be noticeable scars after surgery?

Modern surgical techniques prioritize minimizing scarring. Incisions are strategically placed to be as discreet as possible. With proper post-operative care and Dr. Purohit’s surgical expertise, any scars that do form tend to fade over time, becoming less noticeable.

4. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent. The excess breast tissue and fat are removed, creating a more sculpted and contoured chest. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle through regular exercise and a balanced diet is essential to ensure the longevity of the results.

5. Can gynecomastia be treated without surgery?

Absolutely. Non-surgical treatment options are available, especially for individuals with mild to moderate gynecomastia. Dr. Vishal Purohit offers various non-surgical techniques such as Cryolipolysis and Laser Lipolysis, which can effectively reduce excess fat and enhance the chest’s appearance without the need for surgical intervention.
