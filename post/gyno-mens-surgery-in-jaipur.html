FAQs - Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you consider the transformative journey of gynecomastia surgery, it’s natural to have questions. Here are some commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, along with detailed answers:

1. What is the recovery period after surgery?

The recovery period following gynecomastia surgery typically spans a few weeks. During this time, it’s important to avoid strenuous physical activities that could strain the healing tissues. Following my post-operative instructions diligently will contribute to a smooth recovery and optimal results.

2. Is the surgery performed under general anesthesia?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery is generally performed under the administration of general anesthesia. This ensures that you remain comfortable and pain-free throughout the procedure. Your safety and well-being are paramount, and the choice of anesthesia supports that.

3. Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

While some scarring is inherent to surgical procedures, I employ advanced techniques to minimize the visibility of scars. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas, and over time, scars tend to fade. The focus is on delivering results that look natural and aesthetically pleasing.

4. Are the results of the surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally considered permanent. However, it’s essential to maintain a healthy lifestyle and stable body weight to ensure the longevity of the results. Weight fluctuations and other factors can influence the long-term outcome.

5. Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

In most cases, the results of gynecomastia surgery are long-lasting. However, to minimize the risk of recurrence, it’s advised to maintain a stable weight and adopt a healthy lifestyle. This proactive approach can help you enjoy the benefits of the surgery for years to come.
