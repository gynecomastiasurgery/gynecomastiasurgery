FAQs About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Here, I address some of the commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of the procedure and its implications.

Q: Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

A: Yes, gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a qualified and experienced surgeon like myself. I prioritize patient safety and utilize advanced techniques to minimize risks and complications associated with the procedure.

Q: How long is the recovery period?

A: The recovery period after gynecomastia surgery varies, but most patients can resume light activities within a week. However, full recovery may take a few weeks to a couple of months, depending on individual factors.

Q: Will there be noticeable scars?

A: While there may be small incision scars, I use discreet placement and advanced closure techniques to minimize their visibility. Over time, these scars tend to fade and become less noticeable.

Q: Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

A: In most cases, the results of gynecomastia surgery are permanent. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to prevent any potential recurrence. Gaining excessive weight or using certain medications could potentially influence the outcome.

Q: Are the results of surgery immediately visible?

A: While some initial swelling is common, you should notice a significant improvement in your chest contour shortly after the surgery. As swelling subsides and the healing process progresses, the final results will become more apparent over the following weeks.
