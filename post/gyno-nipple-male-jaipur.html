FAQs: Your Gynecomastia Surgery Questions Answered

1. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Absolutely. Gynecomastia surgery, when performed by a skilled and experienced surgeon, is generally safe. At Glamyo Health, we prioritize your safety and well-being throughout the entire process. Pre-operative assessments and comprehensive aftercare contribute to a successful and secure surgical experience.

2. What is the recovery period after surgery?

Recovery times can vary from person to person. However, most patients can expect to return to their regular activities within a couple of weeks post-surgery. It’s important to follow post-operative instructions carefully and attend follow-up appointments for the best results.

3. Will there be visible scars?

While some scarring is inevitable with any surgical procedure, our techniques aim to minimize visible scars. Any scars that do form are typically discreet and tend to fade over time. We prioritize placing incisions strategically to ensure the most inconspicuous results possible.

4. Can exercise alone eliminate gynecomastia?

Exercise can help tone muscles and improve overall physique, but it may not effectively address glandular tissue, which is often the cause of gynecomastia. Surgical intervention is often required to remove excess breast tissue and achieve a more masculine chest contour.

5. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally long-lasting. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the best possible outcomes over time. Weight fluctuations and hormonal changes can potentially impact the results, so it’s advisable to maintain a stable weight and follow a healthy regimen.
