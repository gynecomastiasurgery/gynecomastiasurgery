FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As a seasoned Mch Plastic Surgeon practicing in Jaipur, I understand that you might have questions and concerns about gynecomastia surgery. To provide you with the information you need, I’ve compiled answers to some of the most commonly asked questions regarding this transformative procedure.

1. What is gynecomastia surgery?

Gynecomastia surgery, also known as male breast reduction surgery, is a procedure aimed at reducing the size of enlarged male breasts. The surgery involves removing excess glandular tissue and, in some cases, fat to achieve a more balanced and masculine chest contour.

2. Is the surgery safe?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery is considered safe when performed by a skilled and experienced plastic surgeon. I have undergone specialized training and possess extensive experience in gynecomastia surgery, ensuring that you’re in capable hands.

3. Am I a candidate for minor gynecomastia surgery?

If you have mild to moderate gynecomastia and are looking to achieve subtle improvements, you might be a candidate for minor gynecomastia surgery. A thorough consultation is necessary to determine the most appropriate approach for your specific case.

4. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery periods can vary depending on the extent of the surgery and individual factors. In most cases, patients can resume light activities within a week. It’s advised to avoid strenuous exercises for about a month to ensure proper healing.

5. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically long-lasting, provided you maintain a healthy lifestyle. However, it’s important to note that significant weight gain or hormonal changes could potentially affect the results over time.
