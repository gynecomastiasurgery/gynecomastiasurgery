Q3: Will there be visible scars after surgery?

Dr. Purohit uses minimally invasive techniques that result in discreet incisions. While there may be minor scarring initially, these typically fade over time and become less noticeable. Dr. Purohit’s emphasis on precision ensures that scarring is kept to a minimum, and he provides guidance on scar care to promote optimal healing.

Q4: Can gynecomastia recur after treatment?

The results of gynecomastia surgery are long-lasting, and the recurrence rate is low. Dr. Purohit provides advice on post-surgery care to help you enjoy lasting results.

Q5: Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. Once excess breast tissue is removed, it won’t grow back. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is key to sustaining the results. Dr. Purohit will guide you on how to preserve the outcome through proper diet, exercise, and overall wellness.
