FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

If you’re considering gynecomastia surgery, it’s natural to have questions. Here are some commonly asked questions to provide you with a better understanding of the procedure and its implications:

1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

Recovery time after gynecomastia surgery varies depending on individual factors and the extent of the procedure. Generally, patients can expect a recovery period of a few weeks. During this time, it’s advised to avoid strenuous activities and follow post-operative care instructions to ensure proper healing.

2. Are the results of gyno surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically long-lasting. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and managing factors that could contribute to gynecomastia, such as hormone imbalances, can help ensure the permanence of the results.

3. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Discomfort is normal after gynecomastia surgery, but any pain can be effectively managed with prescribed medication. During the recovery phase, it’s crucial to follow the prescribed pain management guidelines for a comfortable healing process.

4. Are there any risks associated with the procedure?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries potential risks and complications. During our comprehensive consultation, I will thoroughly discuss these risks with you and address any concerns you may have. My priority is your safety and well-being throughout the entire process.

5. Can I resume regular exercise after recovery?

Gradual re-introduction of exercise is recommended after the recovery period. Light physical activity can aid in the healing process, but it’s important to avoid strenuous exercises and heavy lifting for a few weeks following the surgery. I will provide personalized guidance on when and how to resume exercise safely.
