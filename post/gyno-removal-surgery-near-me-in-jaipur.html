Gynecomastia Surgery Options in Jaipur: Choosing the Right Path

When considering gynecomastia surgery, the range of options available can sometimes be overwhelming. Dr. Vishal Purohit simplifies this process by offering a variety of surgical techniques, each tailored to address specific degrees of gynecomastia and individual patient goals.

1. Liposuction:

  • Ideal Candidates: Liposuction is suitable for patients with mild gynecomastia primarily caused by excess fat accumulation.
  • Procedure: Through small incisions, excess fat is suctioned out, resulting in improved chest contours.
  • Advantages: Minimally invasive, shorter recovery time, minimal scarring.

2. Excision Technique:

  • Ideal Candidates: Patients with more substantial glandular tissue or sagging skin are ideal candidates.
  • Procedure: Surgical removal of excess glandular tissue and skin to achieve a flatter, more masculine chest.
  • Advantages: Effective for moderate to severe gynecomastia, precise sculpting of chest contours.

3. Combination Approach:

  • Ideal Candidates: Some patients benefit from a combination of liposuction and excision for optimal results.
  • Procedure: Dr. Purohit customizes the approach, combining both techniques to address unique concerns.
  • Advantages: Comprehensive correction, tailored to the patient’s specific needs.
