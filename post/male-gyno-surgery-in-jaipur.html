Rest assured that our focus is on making your surgical experience as pain-free as possible.

How long does recovery take after male chest gland removal?

Recovery times can vary depending on the extent of the surgery and individual healing rates. In most cases, patients can resume normal activities within a week, but more strenuous activities and exercises may need to be limited for a few weeks to facilitate proper healing. Dr. Purohit’s team will provide you with detailed post-operative instructions to guide you through a smooth recovery.

Will there be noticeable scars?

Dr. Purohit employs advanced techniques to minimize scarring and ensure a cosmetically pleasing outcome. Gynecomastia surgery is designed to provide long-lasting results. However, it’s important to note that maintaining a healthy lifestyle can play a role in preventing potential recurrences. Adhering to a balanced diet and exercise regimen can help you sustain the positive outcomes of the surgery.

Are the results of the surgery immediately visible?

Following gynecomastia surgery, you’ll notice a significant improvement in your chest contour right away.
