1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

The recovery period following gynecomastia surgery varies from person to person. Generally, patients can expect to return to their regular activities within a few weeks. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous activities during the initial healing phase to ensure optimal results.

2. Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

Dr. Vishal Purohit employs advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous locations to ensure that any resulting scars are discreet and fade over time.

3. Is gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent. Once the excess breast tissue and fat are removed, the improved chest contour should remain stable. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help ensure long-lasting results.

4. Are the results of surgery immediately visible?

While there will be an immediate improvement in the appearance of your chest, it’s important to note that some swelling is normal after surgery. As the swelling subsides and your body adjusts to its new contour, the final results will become more apparent over the coming weeks.

5. What are the potential risks of gynecomastia surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries minimal risks. These can include infection, bleeding, scarring, or uneven results. However, by choosing an experienced surgeon like Dr. Vishal Purohit and following post-operative instructions, these risks can be minimized.
