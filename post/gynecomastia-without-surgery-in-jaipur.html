Types of Gynecomastia Surgery: Gynecomastia surgery involves techniques tailored to the extent of tissue and fat removal needed. Here are some common types:

  • Liposuction: Suitable for cases where excess fat is the primary concern. This technique involves small incisions through which a thin tube (cannula) is inserted to suction out the excess fat.
  • Tissue Excision: Ideal for cases with significant glandular tissue or excess skin. This technique involves making incisions to remove the tissue directly.
  • Combination Approach: In some cases, a combination of liposuction and tissue excision may be used to achieve optimal results.

Customized Treatment: During a consultation, I carefully assess your gynecomastia to determine which surgical technique will yield the best results. I take into account factors such as the amount of excess tissue, fat distribution, and your individual goals to create a personalized treatment plan.
