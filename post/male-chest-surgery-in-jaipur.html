FAQs About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As a plastic surgeon specializing in gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, I understand that you may have questions about the procedure, recovery, and results. Here are answers to some commonly asked questions to help you make an informed decision:

1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

The recovery time can vary based on the extent of the procedure and individual healing factors. Generally, most patients can return to light activities within about a week. However, it’s important to avoid strenuous exercises and heavy lifting for a few weeks to ensure proper healing and optimal results.

2. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

I use advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas to ensure that scars are as discreet as possible. Over time, these scars tend to fade and become less noticeable.

3. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

While some discomfort and soreness are normal after surgery, any pain is typically manageable with prescribed pain medication. I will provide you with detailed post-operative instructions to help you stay comfortable during your recovery.

4. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent as long as a healthy lifestyle is maintained. It’s important to maintain a stable weight and continue practicing a healthy diet and exercise routine to ensure long-lasting results.

5. Are there any risks associated with gynecomastia surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries some risks. These can include infection, bleeding, scarring, and changes in sensation. However, I take every precaution to minimize these risks and ensure a safe and successful surgery. During our consultation, I will discuss the potential risks and how they can be managed.
