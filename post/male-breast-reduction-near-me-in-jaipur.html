FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the path to gynecomastia surgery involves having a clear understanding of the procedure, recovery, and potential outcomes. Here are some commonly asked questions to provide you with the information you need:

Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring a pain-free experience during the procedure itself. After the surgery, it’s natural to experience some discomfort, which is typically managed with prescribed pain medications. Rest assured, my team and I are committed to ensuring your comfort throughout the process.

How long does recovery take?

Recovery duration can vary based on factors such as the extent of surgery and individual healing rates. In general, most patients can resume non-strenuous activities within about a week. However, strenuous activities, such as heavy lifting, should be avoided for approximately a month to ensure proper healing.

Will there be visible scars?

I understand the concern about scarring. Incisions for gynecomastia surgery are strategically placed to minimize visible scarring. Over time, scars tend to fade and become less noticeable. Our meticulous surgical approach aims to achieve both excellent results and discreet scarring.

Are the results permanent?

Gynecomastia surgery provides long-lasting results. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the best possible outcome. Maintaining a stable weight and practicing good overall health habits can help preserve the results achieved through the surgery.

Are there any risks associated with the surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery does carry certain risks. During your consultation, I will discuss the potential risks and complications with you. Rest assured that my team and I prioritize safety and take all necessary precautions to minimize these risks.
