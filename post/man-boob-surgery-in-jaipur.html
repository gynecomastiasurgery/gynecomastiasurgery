Types of Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

When it comes to gynecomastia surgery, it’s important to recognize that each case is unique. As a specialized MCh Plastic Surgeon in Jaipur, I offer a range of surgical techniques tailored to address different forms of gynecomastia. Let’s delve into the various options available to you:

Liposuction for Gynecomastia

Liposuction is a popular and effective technique for treating gynecomastia caused primarily by excess fat deposits in the chest area. During this procedure, small incisions are made, and a thin tube (cannula) is inserted to suction out the excess fat. This results in a more sculpted and defined chest contour. It’s important to note that liposuction works best for cases where there is minimal glandular tissue.

Male Chest Gynecomastia Surgery

For individuals with a combination of excess fat and glandular tissue, male chest gynecomastia surgery might be the appropriate approach. This technique involves both liposuction to remove fat and surgical excision to address the glandular tissue. I carefully tailor the procedure to your unique anatomy, ensuring that you achieve the best possible outcome. This surgery requires precision to create a natural and masculine chest appearance.

Plastic Surgery for Gynecomastia

When it comes to plastic surgery for gynecomastia, my goal is to provide you with results that look and feel as natural as possible. This approach involves advanced surgical techniques that minimize scarring while effectively addressing both fat and glandular tissue. The result is a chest that appears balanced and in harmony with the rest of your body. This procedure is particularly suitable for cases where there is a significant amount of tissue to be removed.

Surgery Type Suitable Candidates
Liposuction for Gynecomastia Patients with excess fat and minimal glandular tissue.
Male Chest Gynecomastia Surgery Individuals with a combination of excess fat and glandular tissue.
Plastic Surgery for Gynecomastia Patients with significant glandular tissue and a need for precise contouring.

The decision on which surgery to opt for depends on factors such as the extent of your gynecomastia, your desired results, and my professional assessment during our consultation.
