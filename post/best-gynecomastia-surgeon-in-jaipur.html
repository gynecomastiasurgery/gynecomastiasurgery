FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Here are the answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur:

1. What is the recovery time after gynecomastia surgery?

Recovery time after gynecomastia surgery can vary depending on individual healing factors and the extent of the procedure. Generally, patients can expect a downtime of about 1 to 2 weeks. During this period, it’s advisable to avoid strenuous physical activities and follow post-operative care instructions for optimal healing.

2. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. Once excess glandular tissue and fat are removed, the transformation is enduring. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, including regular exercise and a balanced diet, can help ensure long-lasting results.

3. Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

I employ advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed to ensure discreet scarring that gradually fades over time. While some scarring is inevitable, it is usually well-concealed and becomes less noticeable as it matures.

4. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

While discomfort is normal after gynecomastia surgery, any pain is typically manageable with prescribed medications. I prioritize patient comfort and provide detailed post-operative care instructions to alleviate discomfort and ensure a smooth recovery.

5. Can I resume physical activities after the surgery?

After a brief initial recovery period, you can gradually resume light physical activities. However, strenuous exercises and heavy lifting should be avoided for several weeks to allow for proper healing. Following post-operative guidelines will help you return to your regular activities safely.
