FAQ’s about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you consider gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s natural to have questions about the procedure, recovery, and results. Here are some commonly asked questions that can help you make an informed decision:

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can result from hormonal imbalances, genetics, obesity, or certain medications. During our consultation, I will assess your individual situation to determine the underlying cause and create a personalized treatment plan.

2. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a qualified plastic surgeon like myself. I prioritize patient safety and employ proven techniques to minimize risks. During your consultation, I will discuss the potential risks and benefits based on your specific case.

3. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery times can vary depending on the extent of the procedure and individual factors. Most patients can expect to resume light activities within a week or two. I will provide you with detailed post-operative instructions to guide you through a smooth recovery process.

4. Will there be visible scars?

I use advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in discreet locations to ensure that scars are as inconspicuous as possible. Over time, scars tend to fade, and I will provide guidance on scar management to optimize healing.

5. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent, provided a healthy lifestyle is maintained. Significant weight fluctuations or hormonal imbalances can affect the long-term outcome, so it’s important to prioritize overall well-being.
