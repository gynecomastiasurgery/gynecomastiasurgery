FAQs: Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Addressing common questions and concerns about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur to help you make well-informed decisions.

What is the ideal age for gynecomastia surgery?

Gynecomastia surgery is typically recommended once the breast tissue has stabilized, which usually occurs in late adolescence or early adulthood. It’s essential to wait until hormonal fluctuations have settled to ensure the most effective and lasting results from the procedure.

Is the procedure painful?

Discomfort is common following gynecomastia surgery, but it can be effectively managed with prescribed pain medications. Most patients describe the discomfort as manageable, and any post-operative pain typically subsides within a few days to a week.

Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

While gynecomastia surgery does involve incisions, the scars are strategically placed to be as discreet as possible. Over time, these scars tend to fade significantly, becoming less noticeable. I take care to place incisions along natural creases and contours to ensure minimal visibility.

How long does the recovery period last?

Recovery duration varies among individuals, but most patients can return to non-strenuous activities within a week. Full recovery and the resumption of more vigorous activities may take around a month. Following post-operative care instructions and attending follow-up appointments contribute to a smoother recovery.

Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Barring significant weight gain or hormonal imbalances, the results of gynecomastia surgery are long-lasting. The excess glandular tissue and fat that cause the enlarged appearance of male breasts are removed, resulting in a more contoured chest. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle helps ensure that the results endure over time.

Question Answer
What causes gynecomastia? Gynecomastia can result from hormonal imbalances, certain medications, obesity, and genetic factors.
Can weight loss resolve gynecomastia? In some cases, weight loss can reduce the appearance of gynecomastia, but surgical intervention may be necessary.
When can I return to work after surgery? Most individuals can return to work within a week, depending on the nature of their job.
How soon can I see the final results? Initial swelling subsides within a few weeks, but it can take several months for final results to become visible.
Is gynecomastia surgery safe? Gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by an experienced surgeon and in a controlled environment.
