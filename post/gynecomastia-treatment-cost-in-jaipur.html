Gynaecomastia Can Be Treated in Jaipur: Exploring the Procedures

Gynecomastia is a condition that can be effectively treated through various surgical techniques, each tailored to address specific concerns and provide optimal results. At our clinic in Jaipur, we offer a range of options to cater to your unique needs and aspirations.

Liposuction Technique: For cases where excess fat is the primary contributor to gynecomastia, the liposuction technique is a valuable option. This minimally invasive procedure involves making small incisions through which a thin tube (cannula) is inserted to suction out excess fat. This technique is particularly effective when the enlarged breast area is predominantly due to fatty tissue.

Excision Technique: When gynecomastia is characterized by excess glandular tissue or sagging skin, the excision technique becomes crucial. This involves making incisions to remove excess tissue and possibly reposition the nipple for a more natural appearance. The excision technique is suitable for cases where the enlargement is not primarily due to fatty tissue and requires a more targeted approach.

Combination Approach: In some instances, a combination of both liposuction and excision techniques may be recommended. This comprehensive approach allows us to address both excess fat and glandular tissue for a more contoured and proportionate chest. During your consultation, we will assess your individual case and recommend the most suitable technique or combination of techniques to achieve your desired outcome.

FAQs: Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the realm of gynecomastia surgery can raise questions, and it’s important to have clear answers to make well-informed decisions. Here are some frequently asked questions that I, Dr. Vishal Purohit, often encounter during consultations.

1. Is the procedure painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you are comfortable throughout the procedure. After the surgery, some degree of discomfort, swelling, and bruising is normal, but these effects are temporary and can be managed with prescribed pain medication. As you progress through the recovery process, any discomfort you experience will gradually subside.

2. Will there be visible scars?

While incisions are made during gynecomastia surgery, they are strategically placed to minimize visible scarring. Over time, the scars tend to fade and become less noticeable. Our team takes great care in ensuring that the incisions are as discreet as possible, while still achieving optimal results.

3. When can I resume regular activities?

Your ability to resume regular activities will depend on the extent of the surgery and your individual healing process. While you’ll need to take it easy during the initial recovery period, light activities such as walking are usually encouraged to promote circulation. More strenuous activities and exercises should be gradually reintroduced as advised by our team.

4. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent, provided you maintain a healthy lifestyle. While the enlarged breast tissue and excess fat are removed during the procedure, it’s important to continue following a balanced diet and exercise regimen to prevent the recurrence of gynecomastia.

5. Is the surgery safe?

Gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a skilled and experienced plastic surgeon in a well-equipped facility.
