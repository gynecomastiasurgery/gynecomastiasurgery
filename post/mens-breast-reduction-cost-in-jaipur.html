The Surgical Process

  5. Surgical Closure: Once the necessary modifications are made, incisions are meticulously closed, often with dissolvable sutures.

FAQs: Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

In our commitment to provide you with comprehensive information, we’ve compiled answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur.

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia, often referred to as “man boobs,” can be caused by various factors, including hormonal imbalances, genetics, obesity, or certain medical conditions. Understanding the underlying cause is crucial in determining the most appropriate treatment approach.

2. Is the procedure painful?

During the procedure, you will be under general anesthesia, ensuring that you are comfortable and pain-free. While discomfort is common during the recovery period, we take measures to manage pain effectively, allowing you to navigate the healing process with ease.

3. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery times can vary based on the extent of the procedure and individual healing rates. In general, most patients can return to light activities within a week or two after surgery. Full recovery may take a few weeks, during which it’s important to follow post-operative care instructions.

4. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. However, it’s important to note that maintaining a healthy lifestyle and weight can contribute to the longevity of the results. Significant weight gain or hormonal imbalances in the future may potentially affect the outcome.

5. Are there any risks involved?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries inherent risks. These can include infection, bleeding, scarring, and unfavorable aesthetic outcomes. However, our experienced surgical team prioritizes safety and takes precautions to minimize these risks. During your consultation, we will discuss potential risks in detail.
